A little about me

I am a computer science graduate from Toronto Metropolitan University. I have always had a deep interest in computer graphics, hardware, and gaming, which lead to me specializing in game / XR programming and graphics during my studies. Since graduating, I have moved on to creating several of my own projects in modern game engines such as Unity and Unreal Engine. I love trying to learn new things so that I can both build upon existing work and expand my skillset for future endeavours.

This website serves as a portfolio to host some of my latest work, as well as projects that I am most proud of. You can click the first social link to view more about my work history on LinkedIn, the second to view my public repositories on GitHub, or the third to play my feature-complete projects on itch.io.

Thank you for visiting my site!
